# Interview process

1. Initial interview with team lead and VPE
  * Cultural fit
  * General chitchat
  * Evaluating communication

2. Technical interview with team lead
  * Technical aptitude
  * Homework assignment assigned

3. Group interview with team
  * Final step to veto
